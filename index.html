Controls

    1. Use WASD or Arrow keys for moving the snake

    2. Press "1" key to play again

    3. Refresh and then Press "2" key to view your last 16 scores.

    4. "3" key to clear your scores.

    5. Use the Esc key for pausing/unpausing

    6. Enable/Disable Eating Sound

    7. Enable/Disable Grid
